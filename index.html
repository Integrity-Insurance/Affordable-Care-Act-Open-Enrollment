Important Enrollment Deadlines


Maryland residents have until January 15, 2026 to enroll in or change their health insurance plans through Maryland Health Connection.

November 1, 2025: The start of Open Enrollment Period

Missing these deadlines means you’ll have to wait until the next open enrollment period, unless you qualify for a Special Enrollment Period due to a life event. It is important to enroll now to ensure continuous coverage.
